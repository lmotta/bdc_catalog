[general]
name:Brazil Data Cube Catalog
description:Plugin to obtain satellite images provided by the Brazil Data Cube (BDC),
            through a query by a spatial extent and a date range.
about:This plugin allows users to query and retrieve satellite scene mosaics
       by defining the collection of interest, a spatial extent, and a date range.

       The images are provided by the Brazil Data Cube (BDC), the plugin retrieves
       them in Cloud Optimized GeoTIFF (COG) format.

       <b>The COG images are only from Brazil</b>

       This plugin is part of the Brazilian Fire Information System (Sisfogo),
       develop by National Center to Prevent and Combat Forest fires (Prevfogo/Ibama).
